About Me

Hello, I am Jade, currently a student in the Interaction
Design Program at Sheridan College. I'm
all about crafting accessible and inclusive user
experiences that feel like your favorite café - warm
and welcoming. But, there's more to me than pixels
and wireframes!

I come armed with a Bachelor's degree in Social Work and a minor in Sociology.
I've collected diverse work experiences like souvenirs on a world tour – from
serving you peanuts as a flight attendant to advocating for kids with disabilities
abroad. I've also delved into the heart of social work, from the gerontology to newcomer agency.
Oh, and I've been the mastermind behind high-volume store displays and even rocked the retail assistant manager gig.
My secret design sauce? I see failure as a launchpad for new possibilities, so I never throw in the towel.
Right now, I'm diving into the wild world of digital illustration – adding some ‘tech pizzazz’
to my art. And when I'm not glued to my screen, I'm a proud mom to three
amazing spawns. You might find me whipping up culinary masterpieces (or, let's be real,
just dinner), crocheting like a pro, or adventuring through Chinese novels.
So, whether we're unraveling a design challenge or trading parenting tales, let's connect and make this journey even more interesting!
